Ricardo Martinez-Garcia

Young Investigator Group Leader, CASUS-HZDR.

Complex-systems researcher interested in understanding the causes and consequences of emergent patterns and collective behaviors in complex systems. In particular, how heterogeneity and stochasticity at the microscopic scale lead to emergent macroscopic order, and how this order influences system-level robustness, resilience, and stability.
